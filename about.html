About Edwin

Edwin Choate is a UX Designer at NCR Corporation in Atlanta. Within the realm of UX, Edwin is especially interested in usability, information architecture, wireframing, and prototyping. He’s also passionate about things like humane technology and issues such as misinformation on the internet and data privacy. In his spare time, Edwin enjoys reading, listening to folk & americana music, retro video games, learning new things, and playing his acoustic guitar.
